The first GDSC Ghana Summit.

Are you a tech student or aspiring developer in Ghana looking to take your career to the next level? Join the Google Developer Student Clubs (GDSC) Ghana for the Career Development Summit! This one-day event is packed with workshops, talks, and networking opportunities designed to equip you with the skills and knowledge you need to succeed in the ever-evolving tech industry. .

Welcome to the inaugural GDSC Ghana Summit! Don't miss your chance to be part of the first edition of this groundbreaking event..
